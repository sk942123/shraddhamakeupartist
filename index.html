Questions

Before you book.

How early should I book bridal makeup?

Popular wedding dates fill quickly, so early booking is recommended once your event date and city are confirmed.

Do you travel to the venue?

Yes. Venue service can be included depending on the location, reporting time and package selected.

Can my look be customised?

Absolutely. Your look is planned around your features, skin type, outfit, jewellery, hairstyle and personal preferences.

Are hairstyling and draping included?

They can be added or bundled depending on your chosen package. Confirm the inclusions while requesting your quote.
